Families Are Suffering. You Can Help Rebuild Hope.

Recent floods have devastated communities across Nigeria, leaving thousands of families homeless and without basic necessities. At APEH-BE Charity, we're on the ground providing immediate relief and long-term support.

Your donation helps us:

  • Rebuild homes and classrooms

  • Restore hope to affected families
  • Support sustainable recovery efforts
