Case Studies & Success Stories

Our clean water initiative in the Village of Hope reached over 500 families, reducing waterborne diseases by 80% and improving overall community health. The project included the installation of solar-powered water pumps and community health education programs.

"Before APEH-BE CHARITABLE FOUNDATION came to our village, we had to walk 3 kilometers to fetch dirty water. Now we have clean water right in our community. Our children are healthier and can focus on their studies." - Maria, Village Elder

Our girls' education program has helped over 200 young women complete their secondary education and pursue higher learning opportunities. The program provides scholarships, mentorship, and life skills training.

"Thanks to APEH-BE CHARITABLE FOUNDATION, I was able to finish school and now I'm studying to become a nurse. I want to help other girls in my community achieve their dreams too." - Amina, Program Graduate

Our agricultural development program has trained over 300 farmers in sustainable farming techniques, resulting in a 150% increase in crop yields and improved food security for their communities.

"The training I received from APEH-BE CHARITABLE FOUNDATION has transformed my farm. I now grow enough food to feed my family and sell the surplus to support my children's education." - John, Farmer
